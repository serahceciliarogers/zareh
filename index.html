ABOUT PANCHLA

Located just about 20 kilometers from the bustling city of Kolkata, Panchla is a vibrant community development block in the Howrah district. Its name, literally translating to "Five Villages," hints at its historical roots as a cluster of settlements that grew together into a thriving hub of culture and commerce.

While the region is framed by the serene alluvial plains of the Damodar and Hooghly rivers, Panchla's true identity lies in its people.

It is a land where agricultural heritage meets industrial skill—a place where the skyline is dotted with both green fields and the busy workshops of master artisans. Today, Panchla stands as a symbol of rural resilience and the timeless beauty of Bengal's traditional crafts.

OUR COMMUNITY

The Fabric of Our Community

Zari Zardozi isn't just a craft here; it's a way of life. The community in Panchla has preserved this intricate art for generations, passing down techniques and passion from parent to child.
